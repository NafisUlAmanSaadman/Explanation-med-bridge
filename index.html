Technical Reference

MedBridge Platform Explanation

An intelligent, progressive, and highly interactive learning system tailored for MBBS students in Bangladesh, strictly aligning with the Bangladesh Medical & Dental Council (BMDC) curriculum.

Problem (The Vibe)

"This problem matters." Medical education is a matter of life and death, yet the learning methods for medical students in Bangladesh are critically broken and outdated.

⚠️

The Local Crisis (Bangladesh)

Over 100,000+ MBBS students across 117 medical colleges in Bangladesh are forced to rely on passive memorization of static PDF slides, outdated photocopied handouts, and unofficial Q&A guides. Faculty shortages, particularly in rural medical colleges, mean students rarely receive high-quality viva guidance or clinical simulation trials.

🌍

The Global Competency Gap

Internationally, medical education has transitioned to high-fidelity clinical simulations, active recall, and Socratic clinical reasoning. Because Bangladeshi students lack interactive simulation tools, they face an uphill battle when transitioning to clinical practice or attempting global licensing exams (like USMLE or PLAB) that test deep clinical application over rote learning.

🚨 The High Stakes: Where the System Fails Students

The BMDC curriculum requires clearing high-stakes oral Viva-Voce and OSPE (Objective Structured Practical Examination) stations. Currently, students have zero ways to practice these dynamic, face-to-face oral exams before exam day. There is no instant feedback loop, creating a massive gap between theoretical knowledge and practical competence.

Solution

"This is how we solve it." MedBridge is a purpose-built, AI-driven learning and assessment bridge that turns passive reading into active, clinical-grade mastery.

🎯

Curriculum-Aligned AI Engines

MedBridge is not a generic study tool. We digitized the entire BMDC syllabus and built interactive AI engines that simulate a strict Bangladeshi medical professor. Our Voice-First AI Viva Engine, OSPE Simulator, and Socratic DDx Trainer force students to explain clinical concepts and perform diagnoses under pressure, with instant grading rubrics.

🔒

The Gatekeeping Progression Engine

We solve student engagement and study discipline by enforcing a strict progressive unlock structure: Item → Card → Term → Prof. Students cannot proceed to larger exam blocks without demonstrating competence (60%+ score) in underlying items via the AI Viva, establishing structured study habits.

✨ Why MedBridge is Different (Our Edge)

Unlike standard LLM integrations, MedBridge uses structured prompt architectures with localized epidemiological contexts (e.g. Bangladesh's seasonal Dengue outbreaks, local hospital protocols), structured output recovery, and local device-level spaced repetition. We combine high-yield clinical simulators with real-time peer duels, creating a highly engaging, localized, and clinical-grade training ground.

Core Interactive Features

🎙️

AI Viva Engine

Simulates real oral viva-voce examinations using native Web Speech APIs. Claude/Gemini prompts 5 clinical/theoretical questions, analyzes the spoken text, and returns structured feedback and grading parameters.

⏱️

OSPE Simulator

Objective Structured Practical Examination simulator. Replicates high-stress exam conditions with automated station advance (2-minute intervals), buzzer/bell warnings, visual references, and post-exam AI rubric checks.

⚔️

Multiplayer Battles

Gamified peer duels. Students join dynamic rooms via 6-character room codes. A Cloud API server generates custom SBA questions, and scores are synced in real-time using Firebase RTDB.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Combines progress scores from Firestore to show "Prof Readiness." Track subject-wise performance graphs (e.g. Physiology vs Biochemistry), log daily study streaks, and locate syllabus bottlenecks.

📰

Medical News Feed

Curated live medical news from Bangladesh (🇧🇩) and international sources. Students stay updated with the latest clinical developments, research breakthroughs, and public health announcements — with search, region-based filtering, and one-tap refresh.

📝

AI Note Summarizer

Paste raw lecture transcripts, textbook paragraphs, or clinical notes, and the Gemini-powered engine instantly generates structured, high-yield bullet-point summaries with heading hierarchy. Includes document scanner (PDF/camera) input, voice playback, and persistent history.

🃏

Flashcard Auto-Generator

Converts dense medical text into interactive Q&A flashcard decks using AI. Features a 3D-flipping card interface with difficulty ratings, "Got It" / "Review Again" self-assessment, progress tracking, and locally saved deck history for spaced repetition.

🩺

Socratic DDx Trainer

An AI-powered clinical reasoning simulator. Students present patient symptoms (or choose from preset clinical cases) and interact with a virtual "Attending Consultant" who uses Socratic questioning to guide differential diagnosis — without spoiling the final answer.

🇧🇩

Bangladesh Endemic Disease DB

A curated database of diseases endemic to Bangladesh — Dengue, Kala-azar, Tuberculosis, Typhoid, Cholera, Arsenicosis, Leptospirosis, and Filariasis. Each entry generates AI-powered clinical case scenarios, investigations, and BMDC-aligned treatment plans on demand.

AI Approach & Intelligence Layer

MedBridge is not a simple quiz app with an LLM bolted on top. Every AI interaction is purpose-engineered with domain-specific prompt architectures, structured output contracts, and personalization feedback loops. Here is how the AI thinking works under the hood.

⚙️ The Prompt Architecture in Practice

Below is a simplified view of how prompts are structured across different features — each one is a standalone AI persona with strict behavioral rules:

Feature AI Persona Output Contract Special Techniques
AI Viva Engine Bangladeshi medical professor Strict JSON: scores[], feedback, total Session seeds, asked-list dedup, phase-aware difficulty
DDx Trainer Senior consultant (Socratic tutor) Formatted Markdown with headings Never reveals diagnosis directly; guides via targeted questions
Flashcard Generator Medical educator JSON array: [{question, answer, difficulty}] Auto-strips markdown fences, JSON boundary recovery
Note Summarizer Clinical note compiler Hierarchical Markdown (H1→H2→bullets) Preserves clinical terminology, structures by system

OSPE Grader Practical exam rubric evaluator Structured rubric JSON Station-specific scoring criteria, image-based assessment
